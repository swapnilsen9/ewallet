About Us


We have built this Platform on the ideology that UI design is one of the most important pillars of any digital service being provided. The first interaction of any user with any of the huge number of digital services is with the UI. The UI plays such a huge role that poor UI design has killed many and several products in the past even though the underlying product had such breakthrough technologies with which no other platform could compete.

So, after reading the last paragraph you might wonder that why did we tell you so much UI or User Interface on the about us section of an E-Wallet Platform. It is kinda confusing. We agree, but if you have read it till here we would like to tell you that our goal is not to compete with other E-Wallet platforms in the traditional sense. Our goal is to provide an experience. An experience which is as clutter free as possible. We love the philosophy of minimalism and we have engraved it in our cores. Each and every element of our platform is designed in a way that the underlying task can be achieved with minimal levels of user interaction and with proper visual feedback on the way.

We want to create a platform which may lack a few advanced features but the user experience can make up for it. The platform must be accessible to a Developer and also to a Technical Noob.
